How to use

  • Assign: click two positions (spaxel or slit slot, any combination) to swap their fibre routing. The 12 dark slots can be moved the same way; only the two calibration fibres are fixed.
  • Mask: click a spaxel or slit slot to toggle its mask. Shift-click does this in any mode.
  • Mask sticks to: when re-routing, the mask pattern either travels with the spaxels (hexagon fixed, slit changes) or stays at its slit positions (slit fixed, hexagon changes).
  • Hovering links the two views; labels on the hexagon show where each spaxel ends up in the slit.
  • Red bars under the slit mark adjacent lit fibres (crosstalk); the goal of the mask is zero.
  • The design autosaves in the browser; use Export / Share URL to keep or send a variant.
